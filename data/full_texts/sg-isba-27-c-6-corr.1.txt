Corrigendum
1. Paragraph 2
For the existing text substitute
2. The present guidelines have been developed to provide practical and technical
guidance on the implementation of an EMMP associated with exploitation of mineral
resources in the Area, as specified in regulations 13 (3) (b), 31, 38, 46, 48, 49, 50, 51,
annex IV, section 11 and annex VII of the exploitation regulations.
2. Paragraph 25, fourth sentence
For the existing text substitute
The mitigation measures identified for each potential Environmental Effect identified
in the EIA should then be used to design the monitoring programme(s) (see below).
3. Paragraph 72
For the existing text substitute
72. Successful completion of monitoring activities requires the proper training of
personnel, and the EMMP should therefore contain clearly described training
protocols. This can be included in the overall training for the site, which is
summarized below.
